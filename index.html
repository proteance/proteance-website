The Challenge

Too many dealerships are held back by systems that were never designed to work together. OEM portals, DMS, CRM, finance — each doing their own thing, with staff manually bridging the gaps every day. Meanwhile, deals are lost to competitors and no one knows exactly why.

At Proteance, we believe your systems should support your processes — not dictate them, and not create work. We build the integration layer that connects what you have, and the intelligence tools that tell you what's not working.

DIBOP

Dealer Integration Backbone & Orchestration Platform

The integration spine that sits between your OEM APIs, DMS, CRM, and finance systems. Connect each system once — data moves automatically, business logic lives in one place, and your team stops re-entering what should sync itself.
