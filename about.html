About Proteance

Strategic technology and business consultancy for modern dealerships

Who We Are

Proteance is a strategic technology and business consultancy focused on modernizing dealership and customer engagement systems. We specialize in contact center transformation, CRM integration, and AI-powered service automation.

Our team brings together deep experience in enterprise-scale operations, dealership systems, and OEM strategy—bridging the gap between legacy DMS environments and cloud-native, Microsoft-driven platforms like Dynamics 365.

At Proteance, we don't just know how to deploy Dynamics—we know how to make it work for dealerships, with all the complexity, operational nuance, and customer expectations that come with them.
